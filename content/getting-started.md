New to mesh? Start here. You'll have a node on the air in an afternoon.

## 1. Get a radio

Almost any MeshCore-compatible LoRa board works. Good first nodes for the
915 MHz US band:

- **Heltec T114** - cheap, easy to flash, low power, great for a first client node.
- **Heltec V3** - cheap, easy to flash, great for a first client node.
- **RAK WisBlock (RAK4631)** - low power, ideal for solar repeaters.
- **Seeed T1000-E** - pocketable tracker-style client.

## 2. Flash MeshCore

Flash the latest MeshCore firmware for your board, then set the regional
parameters so you land on the OKI Mesh:

**Preset:** USA/Canada (Recommended)
 
**Byte Count:** 2-Bytes


## 3. Say hello

Power up, join the public channel, and post in our Discord so we can confirm
you're being heard. Welcome to the mesh.
